Privacy Policy

Effective: [2025-09-03]

This policy explains how the “Receipt Processor” application (the “App”) used by Generations Church handles information.

Data We Process

  • QuickBooks Online data required for bookkeeping (e.g., accounts, vendors, transactions).
  • Receipt files you upload (images/PDFs) and derived fields (subtotal, taxes, categories).
  • Authentication tokens necessary to access your QuickBooks company.

How We Use Data

Solely to automate receipt processing and maintain accurate accounting records inside your QuickBooks company.

Storage & Security

  • Access tokens are short‑lived; refresh tokens are stored securely on authorized devices only.
  • Receipt files remain in storage controlled by Generations Church (e.g., local drive or church cloud storage).

Sharing

We do not sell or share data with third parties. Data flows only between the App and Intuit QuickBooks APIs.

Retention & Deletion

Data is retained only as needed for bookkeeping and compliance. To request deletion of locally stored data or to revoke API access, contact us or disconnect the App in QuickBooks.
